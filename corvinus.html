The Story of the Team

In 2020 Corvinus University of Budapest went through a complete digitalization project, part of this was a new application designed to help students, teachers and visitors getting structured and relevant information about the university and many more.

Teams of BitRaptors, Corvinus and IncepTech worked closely together as one group with from brainstorm and kick-off state all the way to the release process and future developments, bonding a strong connection among all the participants.

The Giant Scope

University has lots of fragmented data from various sources, which often can only be accessed, from non-mobile-friendly sites. While designing the application, we focused on three high-level usecases of our users, divided into three fragments:

As a student, I want to follow my classes, see where they take place and get all my details related to my education, smoothly and natively from my phone. As a teacher, I want a way to communicate with my students, which can be treated as a single source of truth channel.
