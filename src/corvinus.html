The Story of the Team

In 2020 Corvinus University of Budapest went through a complete digitalization project lead by IncepTech, part of this was a new application designed to help students, teachers and visitors getting structured and relevant information about the university and many more.

Teams of BitRaptors, Corvinus and IncepTech worked closely together as one group with from brainstorm and kick-off state all the way to the release process and future developments, bonding a strong connection among all the participants.

The Giant Scope

University has lots of fragmented data from various sources, which often can only be accessed, from non-mobile-friendly sites. While designing the application, we focused on three high-level usecases of our users, divided into three fragments:

As a student
I want to follow my classes, see where they take place and get all my details related to my education, smoothly and natively from my phone.

As a teacher
I want a way to communicate with my students, which can be treated as a single source of truth channel.

As a visitor
I want to understand the most about Covinus University, read its most recent news and stories.

The Agile Method

We have worked in the smallest possible feedback loop with Corvinus and its students, doing user research with 1:1 interviews and questionaires, also usertests with clickable prototypes.
Once we figured out the high level structure of the app, development started, and iterations continued focusing on more and more detailed questions.

The Dashboard

Which has it all

After signing up with the university credentials, students can see all their information collected on the Dashboard.
This consist two area: The Feed, a curated section of their upcoming classes, news and other useful information, like the opening time of the study department
Underneath the feed students can find a more structured area, containing a collection of the most important informations regarding their studies: their active classes, grade point averages, upcoming exams and results, teachers with their contact informations and a cummulated area in the top of their overall progress in their studies (using the credit system)

🏫

The Timetable

No surprieses, students can find their timetable in this area.
Though we didn't stop here, We wanted to show an overview of how their week looks like, students can see, which are the busiest days in their week, by opening the calendar and looking at the dots representing classes.
They can also open each class and get relevant information collected by their teachers.
Students can track their attendance, so they can avoid failing due to low attendance. They can also save notes for their classes.

🗞️ 📰

The News

The news section is the main platform for the university to communicate with their students and also outside visitors in a structured way.
Users can filter articles based on the topic, wether it's important information, like opening times during holidays, or general news regarding the university. They can also share these articles, or save them, so it's easy to find them next time.
